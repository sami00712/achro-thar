About Achro Thar

Achro Thar, also known as the White Desert, is one of Pakistan's most unique and untouched natural wonders. Located in the Sindh province, between the districts of Sanghar and Khairpur, this mesmerizing white sand desert is home to rare landscapes, rich culture, and tranquil beauty that remains largely unexplored.

What sets Achro Thar apart is not only its stunning white dunes but also its beautiful lakes, such as the Salt Lake and Botar Lake, which add a surreal charm to the region. These natural water bodies offer peaceful views and a refreshing contrast to the desert environment.

Our Mission

Our goal is to showcase the real beauty of Achro Thar to the world – its scenery, its people, and its cultural depth. Through this platform, we aim to:

  • Promote sustainable tourism in Achro Thar
  • Share detailed insights about its landscapes, especially Salt Lake and Botar Lake
  • Highlight the local lifestyle, traditions, and hospitality
  • Encourage eco-friendly exploration and support for local communities

Whether you're a traveler, a nature lover, or a cultural explorer – Achro Thar promises an unforgettable experience. Let us guide you through the hidden gems of Pakistan's White Desert.
